Introduction:

Hello, my name is Kegan David Morin. I am 21 years old. I love playing video games and play all kinds of card games. growing up I have been surrounded by technology and it always has made me happy when I am playing my favorite game. I want to make a game like that for someone else one day. I am a really hard worker and always give it my best and can work really well with others. Below are some of the projects that I have been working on the past few years and will be continuing to work on as the years go by.

Artifact Two:

This is a Unity Game project I have been working on over the past few years. I call the game Ageless and it is about using age to solve puzzles and complete platforming levels while exlporing the wonders of age and growing up. I plan to work on this and make it into a full fledged game into the future and has taught me a whole lot about the unity game engine as a whole. It started as a VR game project which gave me someone experience working in VR as well as standard 3D game development. Some of the models seen are free sourced but most of the ojects were 3d modeled myself and will continue to make more interesting puzzles and worlds as time goes on

Artifact One:

This artifact is a project I did working with the company Torchfire on making concept art for a game they are designing.
These concepts depict dirt and mud huts covered in moss and have various objects around them depicting the professions of the people that lived in them. The project gave me lots of experience with creating concept art and images as well as actual company experience signing an NDA with them and communicating regularly to identify what the company was looking for.

Artifact Three:

This is an AR shooter game in which there is an alien invasion coming from another world through portals and your job to to denfend your home and collect candies before the aliens destroy your home and take over the world. It was a group project where I worked with three other people to create an AR game with none of us having much knowledge or experience before getting together. I had a large focus in the art, animations, and particle effects and had given assistance on the AR and programming side of the project as well. This was a fun project to do with our group and we had fun with our idea and made ourselves laugh the whole time as we were putting it together and for my first AR game I am pretty glad at how it turned out.
